## Option 2: Markdown

**Best for**: Technical users, developers, detailed formatting

**Features:**
- Full markdown rendering
- Headers, bold, lists, etc.
- Most detailed formatting
- Familiar to developers

## Option 3: Simple Text

**Best for**: Quick reading, minimal formatting, accessibility

**Features:**
- Plain text with line breaks
- No special formatting
- Fastest to render
- Most accessible

**Example Output:**
```
Amazon Stock News Summary

Current Status: Amazon (AMZN) is trading at $250.20, up 0.35% today, near its 52-week high of $258.60.

Key Recent Developments:

Major Cloud Wins:
- $38 billion OpenAI partnership - 7-year AWS deal for AI computing infrastructure
- $5.5 billion Cipher Mining deal - 15-year lease for AI workload infrastructure

Stock Performance:
- Stock up 4% recently following positive earnings and analyst upgrades
- AWS cloud growth calmed investor fears about AI investment returns
```

---

## How to Switch

Look for the dropdown selector at the top-right of the chat interface:
- **Structured (Recommended)** - Default, best for most users
- **Markdown** - Full formatting for power users
- **Simple Text** - Plain text, no formatting

The setting applies to all messages in the current session!
